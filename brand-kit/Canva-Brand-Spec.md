# Friederike Altmann · Canva Brand Spec

For all Instagram posts (1080 × 1350) and stories (1080 × 1920) in Canva.

---

## 2. Visual rules (apply everywhere)

1. **Always Manrope.** Never Arial, Calibri, or Open Sans.
2. **Exactly one saffran-accent word** per post. The keyword that earns the highlight is the punchline. The rest stays Tinte (dark mode: Off-White).
3. **Eyebrow tag** at top: tiny ALL CAPS, letter-spacing `.26em`, color Nachtflieder on light · Flieder 200 on dark.
4. **Small radius** on every container: 8 px corners. No pill shapes.
5. **Saffran rule** as visual punctuation: a 40 px × 3 px rectangle (≈ "—") placed wherever you'd use a divider. Saffran color.
6. **Signet placement:** top-left at 80 × 80 px on most posts, or footer-right at 60 × 60 px on data-heavy posts. Use the variant that contrasts with the background.
7. **Footer line:** "FRIEDERIKEALTMANN.DE" in tiny ALL CAPS, letter-spacing `.3em`, Nachtflieder/Lilac.
8. **Generous whitespace.** 80 px outer margins minimum. Editorial calm, not crammed.
9. **No stock illustrations.** Use the three brand sketches (Einzel · Prozess · Gruppe) as the only decorative line-art, low opacity (~25 %).
10. **No emojis** in templates.

## 3. Template specs

### 3.1 EDUCATION · Stat Card (dark)

- **Canvas:** 1080 × 1350, background Tinte `#3A3142`
- **Signet:** top-left, 80 × 80 px, light variant
- **Eyebrow:** `ZAHLEN · DATEN · FAKTEN` · Manrope Bold 24pt · Flieder 200
- **Saffran vertical rule:** 4 px × 200 px, left of the number
- **Big number:** Manrope ExtraBold 200pt · Saffran · letter-spacing −0.05em · e.g. "80 %"
- **Sentence below:** Manrope Regular 30pt · Off-White · line-height 1.4
- **Source line at bottom:** Manrope Bold 16pt ALL CAPS letter-spacing .25em · Lilac Muted
- **Footer URL:** Manrope Bold 14pt ALL CAPS letter-spacing .3em · Lilac Muted

### 3.2 EDUCATION · Tipp (light)

- **Canvas:** 1080 × 1350, background Off-White `#FCFBFA`
- **Signet:** top-left, 80 × 80 px, dark variant
- **Eyebrow:** `RESILIENZ · TIPP 01` · Manrope Bold 24pt · Nachtflieder
- **Headline:** Manrope ExtraBold 72pt · Tinte · letter-spacing −0.04em · 2–3 lines · ONE word in Saffran (italic)
- **Sub-line:** Manrope Bold 32pt · Tinte
- **Body paragraph:** Manrope Regular 22pt · Tinte · line-height 1.5
- **Saffran rule** (40 × 3 px) just above the footer
- **Footer URL** as spec rule 7

### 3.3 ENCOURAGEMENT · Zitat (lilac)

- **Canvas:** 1080 × 1350, background Flieder 50 `#F9F7FA`
- **Signet:** top-left, 80 × 80 px, dark variant
- **Eyebrow:** `EIN KERNSATZ` · Nachtflieder
- **Decorative " mark:** Georgia/serif 320pt · Saffran · top-left of quote
- **Quote:** Manrope ExtraBold Italic 60pt · Tinte · 2 lines max · ONE word in Saffran italic
- **Saffran rule** below the quote (60 × 3 px)
- **Attribution:** Manrope Bold 18pt ALL CAPS letter-spacing .25em · "FRIEDERIKE ALTMANN" · Tinte
- **Sub-attribution:** Manrope Italic 18pt · "Kernsatz meiner Arbeit" · Nachtflieder

### 3.4 ENTERTAINMENT · Mama-Alltag (warm)

- **Canvas:** 1080 × 1350, background Sand `#F1EDE6`
- **Photo placeholder:** top 60% of canvas, 8 px rounded corners, full-width
- **Eyebrow (above photo on dark overlay):** `MAMA-ALLTAG · BEHIND THE SCENES` · Off-White
- **Headline (below photo):** Manrope ExtraBold 48pt · Tinte · ONE word in Saffran
- **Caption:** Manrope Regular 22pt · Tinte · 2 lines max
- **Signet:** footer-right, 60 × 60 px, dark variant
- **No saffran rule** here — the photo is the visual anchor

### 3.5 CAROUSEL · Cover (dark, bold typo)

- **Canvas:** 1080 × 1350, background Tinte `#3A3142`
- **Signet:** top-left, 80 × 80 px, light variant

- **Headline (the cover hook):** Manrope ExtraBold 96pt · Off-White · letter-spacing −0.045em · line-height 0.95 · 3 lines · ONE word in Saffran
- **Sub-line:** Manrope Italic 26pt · Flieder 200 · "Wische für 5 Tools →"
- **Saffran rule + footer URL** at bottom

### 3.6 STORY · Frage (vertical)

- **Canvas:** 1080 × 1920, background Flieder 100 `#EEE9F1`
- **Top 240 px:** IG safe zone — keep empty
- **Bottom 380 px:** IG safe zone — keep empty
- **Signet:** top-left within safe zone, 90 × 90 px, dark variant
- **Eyebrow:** `STORY-FRAGE` · Nachtflieder
- **Headline (the question):** Manrope ExtraBold 90pt · Tinte · letter-spacing −0.04em · 3–4 lines · ONE word in Saffran

- **Question-sticker zone:** rounded rectangle 90% wide × 200 px tall, dashed border 3 px Saffran, labeled "→ Frage-Sticker hier"

## 4. Voice (paste into Canva note for each template)

- Warm, on eye-level, never preachy.
- Tagline: **Entspannt erfolgreich.**
- Core message: **Resilienz ist trainierbar — und kein Schicksal.**
- German Du form throughout; never "Sie".
- One idea per post. If you need two, make it a carousel.

---

## 5. Workflow once the brand kit is set up

1. New Canva design → pick canvas size (1080 × 1350 or 1080 × 1920).
2. Apply Brand Kit (sidebar → Brand).
3. Drop signet from Brand uploads.
4. Type content following the template spec above.
5. Save as Brand Template so the next post starts from the right shell.
